Travel & Holidays


  • Complete Digital Solution
  • June 2020
Other :
  • Expert Engagement
  • Milk Delivery
  • Cylinder Supply

Client Need: The client is in the business of travel management. The operations include taking care of travel booking, hotel booking, and planning/arrangement of various activities/visits during the holiday. The requirement consists of two portals wherein the first one is a dynamic website showcasing various holiday packages from various destinations. The user can explore and select the travel package. The portal also facilitates the user to design the customized itinerary as per his choice. The user can post the travel blogs on this portal. Write reviews and also can post photographs to showcase the experience. The portal also has a knowledge base of information of various tourist destinations worldwide, which can be referred to as a travel guide. The second portal is a content management system that is used for the management of the first portal. This portal is used by the client’s back-end/management team where they can manage the bookings and add the content into the system.
