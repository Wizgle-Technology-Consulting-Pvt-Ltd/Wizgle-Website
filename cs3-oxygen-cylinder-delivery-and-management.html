Oxygen Cylinder Delivery & Management


  • Supply Chain and Delivery Management
  • July 2020
Other :
  • Travel & Holidays
  • Expert Engagement
  • Milk Delivery

Client Need: The client is a medical oxygen cylinder distributor and requires a QR-code based cylinder tracking and management system that will take care of sales and stock management. The application can be used as a back office cms portal which should maintain the details of in stock, in service and out of service oxygen cylinders. Along with that the same portal should be capable of managing customer orders and can be used as point of sales.

Wizgle Solution: The solution consists of an integrated web portal that can be used as content management/Order management and a point of sale as well. All the details should be maintained and all the cylinder related activities should be recorded on the scanning of QR code placed on the cylinder. The activities include to place an new order, return the empty cylinder or to mark the cylinder as disputed (No longer in service).
